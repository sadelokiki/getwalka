How a walk becomes who you are.

Habits aren't built on the good days. They're built on the days you'd normally skip. And because Walka includes grace days, one bad Tuesday can't end the whole thing. Perfection was never the goal. Finishing is.

How it works

Two ways to hold yourself to it.

✦ Bet on Yourself

Just you and the sidewalk. Set a personal goal, put money on the line, and earn it back when you finish.

  1. Choose your walk

    A 5K stroll or a 12K trek. Pick the daily distance, how many days, and what you're committing.

  2. Get out the door

    Your phone counts the walk for you. No logging, no fudging. And grace days are built in, for the days life wins.

  3. Finish and earn it back

    Walk your days and every dollar comes home with you. It was never about the money. It's about who kept it.

⚑ Group Pool

Walk together, even apart. Join a small group, commit money, and earn bonus rewards from those who don't finish.

  1. Open a pool

    Invite up to 8 people you trust: the friends who'll notice if your day goes quiet on the board.

  2. Walk your own way

    Morning loops, lunch breaks, evening strolls. The board updates daily, and showing up is visible.

  3. Finishers split the pot

    Complete the challenge and win more than you put in, funded by the ones who stopped. This time, that's not you.
